Rain Light Fade Tackles Deep Subjects on Slaves and Sinners - NorthWest Music Scene

A Seattle-area rock band's new CD takes aim at a very heady topic: the trafficking of children for sexual exploitation. The songs on Rain Light Fade 's 'Slaves and Sinners' also tackle what singer and keyboardist Dane Vance Creek calls "the objectification of women and the culture that breeds it., but so often refuses to acknowledge that it's even happening."
